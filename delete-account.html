Account deletion

Request deletion of a Protocol Pal account.

If save accounts are enabled for your app version, email support@protocolpal.app from the address on your account.

What to include

Include your account email and a clear request to delete your Protocol Pal account and associated cloud backup data.

Local-only data

If you never created a save account, your data may exist only on your device. Deleting the app or clearing local app data may remove local records.
