Privacy first

Your protocol data should feel like a private health notebook, not an ad profile.

Protocol Pal starts as a private tracker. The website does not require a login, does not expose private keys, and does not connect to any medical account or backend from this landing page.

Future save-account features should use only public frontend configuration, with private service keys kept off the client.
