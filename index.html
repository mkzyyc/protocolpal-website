GLP-1, peptide, medication, supplement, and protocol tracking

Built around the full protocol workflow, from first dose to provider-ready history.

Protocol Pal combines the tracker, calculator, compound library, inventory log, lab journal, and reporting tools people usually have scattered across notes, spreadsheets, and separate websites.

Track & Log

Doses, ramps, cycles, sites, and side effects.

  • One-tap quick logs or detailed entries with amount, unit, time, site, and notes.
  • Multi-phase titration schedules for dose escalation, maintenance, pauses, and restarts.
  • On/off cycle tracking, missed-dose review, site rotation, reactions, and side-effect notes.
  • Reminder templates for daily, weekly, interval, and custom protocol schedules.
Inventory & Supplies

Know what is in stock before your protocol breaks.

  • Track vials, pens, bottles, sprays, topicals, orals, kits, and supplies.
  • Save strength, volume, batch, expiry, open date, storage, cost, and supplier notes.
  • Monitor BAC water volume, punctures, usage history, and open-vial shelf-life clocks.
  • Use low-stock, expiration, reorder, and auto-deduct logic tied to dose logging.
Analyze & Visualize

See adherence, levels, trends, and what changed.

  • Half-life curves, concentration modeling, steady-state estimates, and protocol timelines.
  • Adherence heatmaps, streaks, missed-dose gaps, and dose history filters.
  • Correlation views for treatments, weight, glucose, blood pressure, HRV, labs, and custom metrics.
  • Snapshot sharing and exports for coaching, clinician visits, or personal review.
Health & Reports

Labs, markers, photos, and exports in one place.

  • Bloodwork tracking with standard or custom ranges and marker-by-marker notes.
  • CSV-style lab history, custom health metrics, unit handling, and progress photos.
  • Weekly protocol reviews that summarize dosing, missed steps, inventory, labs, and next actions.
  • PDF, CSV, and text-ready report surfaces for a cleaner medical or coaching conversation.
