Privacy policy

Protocol Pal is built for private protocol tracking.

Last updated: July 1, 2026

What this website collects

This marketing website does not require an account and does not ask for protocol, medical, dosing, lab, or payment details.

App data

Protocol Pal is intended to store private protocol records inside the app unless you choose a future save-account or backup feature. Do not send sensitive health information by email unless you are comfortable doing so.

Cloud backup

If optional cloud backup is added later, the app will describe what is stored and what account provider is used before you enable it. Private service keys must not be placed in frontend code.
